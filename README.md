# Life in Months

## Visualize Your Life in Months

This project helps you visualize a 90-year human life in months. Each dot represents a month of your life. Reflect on the time you have lived and let it inspire you to make the most of every moment.

### How to Use

1. Select your birthdate using the date picker.
2. Click the "Show Lived Months" button to see how many months you have lived so far.
3. Use this visualization as a reminder to cherish each moment and strive towards your goals.

### What Will You Do With Your Time?

Use this tool to reflect on your life and set meaningful goals for the future. Every month is an opportunity to grow, learn, and achieve something great. Time is flying by – don't procrastinate.

### Why Visualize Your Life in Months?

This tool helps you understand the finite nature of time and encourages you to make the most of every moment. By visualizing your life in months, you can see how much time you have lived and how much time you have left to pursue your dreams, make memories, and impact the world.
